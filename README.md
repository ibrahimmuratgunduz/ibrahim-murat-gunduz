## About

Ibrahim Murat Gunduz is a Turkish nationalist and international sports administrator involved in global combat sports governance.

He serves as Vice President of the International Federation of Muaythai Associations (IFMA), the world governing body for Muaythai, and is also a member of the Advisory Board of the Turkish Karate Federation where he contributes as Sponsorship Advisor.
